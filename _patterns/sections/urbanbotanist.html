The Urban Botanist

Infographics

DESCRIPTION: The Urban Botanist is an Ottawa based business that provides fun terrarium workshops. They share their knowledge about terrariums and ecosystems while being able to create your own terrarium. The business was started to share their passion to educate others about the world of botany in an urban setting.

NEEDS: The client needed infographics that matches her personality and style to show consistency throughout her whole brand. Colours and font were chosen carefully to depict the purpose of the brand.
