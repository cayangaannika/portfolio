Catholic Education Foundation of Ottawa

Branding

DESCRIPTION: The Catholic Education Foundation of Ottawa raise funds to help alleviate poverty for students in the Ottawa Catholic School Board. This organization also provides for families who are experiencing financial crisis and can’t afford basic needs such as food, clothing and transportation. By donating to this organization, it can have an immediate impact on the life of a child. Donation in any amount can contribute towards putting food on the table, clothing for the winter or helping a family with financial issues. By doing so, this will influence a child’s education for the better.

NEEDS: The original logo has potential but improvements could be made to create a more simple and dynamic feel. Simple changes such as the colour palette and shape will benefit the overall look of the brand and its purpose. Using light bold colours and dynamic shapes will help customers remember what the brand exists for. Looking at the original logo by itself, customers may get confused on what the logo is trying to portray. Adding the abbreviation of the charitie’s name will help enhance the uniqueness of their brand.
