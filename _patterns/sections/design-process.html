My Design Process

Research Stage

Observe, define and assess client needs by researching competitors and gathering data on target market to have a better understanding of the project.

Brainstorming and Sketching Stage

This is the period when I ideate & develop rough drafts and sketches from the collected data research.

Prototyping stage

Plan & deliver by creating mock ups as close to the final product as possible. Collected information from stage 1 & 2 will be used to create a prototype.

Testing and producing stage

The final stage is to evaluate the prototype by conducting some tests with real users and to revise as needed.
