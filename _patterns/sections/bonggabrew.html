Bongga Brew

Branding and Package design

DESCRIPTION: Bongga Brew is a fictional beer brand that is inspired by my Filipino culture. The graphics and design of the brand will have feminine elements. Having a masculine font type for the logo will balance the tone of the brand. Some components of the brand will have retro/vintage notes to showcase my style. The goal is to create a beer brand that is beer-friendly to all sexes. Targeting this big mass of people will allow this brand to profit from its revenue. This brand will have flavour profiles that are made for each season of the year: Spring, Summer, Winter and Fall. The colour theme of the brand will be based on these 4 seasons.

NEEDS: A brand identity that speaks and is inclusive to women since the beer world is targeted more towards men.

Inspiration

The logo consists of some Filipino elements such as the 3 stars and the shape of a triangle that are shown on the flag. I have transcribed those components into the logo to show that it is filipino inspired. The flavour profiles will also be Filipino fruits inspired to showcase the diversity of the Filipino culture. The name of the brand derived from a filipino word “Bongga” which defines as stylish, lavish, fancy, flamboyant. The shape of the flag is also used as inspiration for the shape of the beer label as shown on the right.
